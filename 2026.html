A note from Igor

Ten years is worth more than a photo, but a photo is where the memory lives.

Hi Seth, thank you for reaching out, and congratulations on your tenth anniversary. There's something extraordinary about a couple who chooses to mark a decade together with a trip across the world. That intentionality is exactly what I love to photograph.

The area around Westminster is one I know well, and I've found over years of shooting in London that the best images usually happen a street or two away from where the crowds are. I know the light there, the quieter alleys off the South Bank, the riverside angles that most visitors never find.

My job is to make you both feel completely at ease, not posed, not directed into uncomfortable stances, just yourselves, in one of the world's most beautiful cities. The images take care of themselves from there.

What to expect

01
Before we meet

We'll connect to talk through what you're hoping for. I'll suggest specific spots based on the time of day and the light forecast. London in late May gives us beautiful golden hour light from around 8pm, and I want to make the most of every minute.

02
The session itself

No stiff posing. I'll guide you through natural movement, a walk, a glance, a laugh. Most couples are surprised by how quickly they forget the camera is there. That's when the real pictures happen.

03
Your gallery

Fully edited, colour-graded images delivered via a private online gallery. Every image is finished to the same high standard. Download at full resolution, share privately, or order prints directly.
